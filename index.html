Publication standards

Pressure comes from verifiable records, not harassment.

01

Fact mapping

Public claims should be tied to records, correspondence, filings, receipts, photographs, or clearly labeled personal opinion.

02

Right of reply

Targets and readers can send corrections, responses, and update requests to the correction address listed on every page.

03

Privacy limits

Pages should not publish home addresses, private contact details, financial account data, medical data, or unrelated personal information.
